About Us

What Inspired Us?

During the lockdown period, we realised that not everyone has the chance to obtain one-to-one guidance from teachers on topics that they may struggle with.

Our Aim

We aim to help students who are currently struggling with their academics due to the disruption of classes and lack of access to affordable tuition classes during this challenging time.

Who Are We?

We are a team of Malaysian youths hoping to help SPM students to achieve their best.

How It Works?


Who can apply as tuttee?

Those who are form 5 and taking SPM

How many subjects can I sign up for?

As a student, you may sign up for one subject per month. If there are more than one subjects that you struggle with, you may sign up for a different subject during a different term.

Who can apply as a tutor?

If you fulfil the following criteria, you may apply as a tutor:
1. Graduated from the SPM syllabus
2. Obtained a minimum grade A in SPM for the subjects that you wish to tutor
3. Graduated from secondary school less than 5 years ago

What are the responsibilities of a volunteer tutor?

Throughout the term that they have signed up for, tutors are expected to conduct weekly, one-hour tutoring sessions via free online platforms such as Zoom. Tutors are also free to set the date and time for each of the tutoring sessions.

If I wish to continue as a tutor/ tutee for another term, do I have to sign up again?

Yes. This is because the pairings of tutors and tutees will be renewed every month. This is to adhere to the one-month commitment policy for tutors and to allow tutees to sign up for a different subject during a new term.
